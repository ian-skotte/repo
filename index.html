Premium

The amount paid each month to keep coverage active. For State and Higher Education, this tool uses the employee premium shown in the premium chart.

Deductible

The amount you pay for eligible expenses before the plan starts sharing certain costs. Some copays and preventive services may work differently.

Out-of-pocket maximum

The most you should pay for covered eligible in-network medical, behavioral health and non-specialty pharmacy costs during the year, excluding premiums.

This prototype focuses on 2026 active employee medical/health plans. It intentionally leaves dental, vision, life and disability out of the main comparison to keep the experience clear.
