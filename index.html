Premium

The regular cost of coverage. In this tool, the employee monthly cost is an estimate after the employer or agency contribution is applied.

Deductible

The amount you pay for certain covered services before the plan starts paying. Some services may be covered before the deductible is met.

Out-of-pocket maximum

The most you would pay for covered in-network medical costs during the plan year, not including premiums.
