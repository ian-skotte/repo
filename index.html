Premium

The monthly cost for coverage before any employer or agency contribution is applied.

Deductible

The amount you may pay for certain covered services before the plan starts paying. Some services may be covered before the deductible is met.

Out-of-pocket maximum

The most you pay for covered services during the plan year. Premiums and some non-covered costs do not count toward this amount.

Annual cost estimate

For health plans, this combines the employee’s annual premium share with the in-network medical out-of-pocket maximum.
