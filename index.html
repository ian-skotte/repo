Premium

The regular cost of health coverage. In this tool, the employee monthly cost is an estimate after the employer or agency contribution is applied.

Deductible

The amount you pay for certain covered medical services before the plan starts paying. Some services may be covered before the deductible is met.

Out-of-pocket maximum

The most you would pay for covered in-network medical costs during the plan year, not including premiums.

Important note

This tool is for health plan comparison only. It does not replace official plan documents, agency benefits coordinator guidance or payroll confirmation. Local Education and Local Government estimates depend on the contribution amount entered. Premiums, deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums are based on the uploaded 2026 Partners for Health comparison spreadsheets.
