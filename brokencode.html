What the Results Mean

Column What it means Calculation used
Partners Premium Rate The full monthly premium rate for one employee. Rate from the premium table
Agency Contribution The agency share entered as either a percentage or dollar amount per employee. User-entered value
Agency Monthly Cost (Total) The total estimated amount the agency pays each month for that employee group. Agency contribution amount per employee × Employees
Employee Monthly Cost (Per Employee) The estimated amount each employee pays per month. Partners Premium Rate − Agency contribution amount per employee
Agency Annual Cost (Total) The total estimated amount the agency pays per year for that employee group. Agency Monthly Cost × 12
Employee Annual Cost (Per Employee) The estimated amount each employee pays per year. Employee Monthly Cost × 12
